Everything is so Danceable!

Welcome to my final project for SP26 DSC80. My name is Corey Ngo. I will cover popular tracks and train a model on techno songs.

Introduction

The dataset I am working with is Spotify metadata for songs ranging from 1927 to 2023. I am studying what makes a song popular, particularly techno tracks.

As a techno DJ, understanding crowd response is essential to maintaining energy and engagement.

Data Cleaning and Exploratory Analysis

Data Cleaning

Converted release_date to datetime using pd.to_datetime(). Replaced invalid keys (-1) with NaN. Removed duplicate tracks by keeping the highest popularity version.

Univariate Analysis

Techno tracks are highly danceable, mostly between 0.6 and 0.8, showing a left-skewed distribution.
